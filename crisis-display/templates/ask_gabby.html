Dear Gabby,

My boyfriend keeps leaving his socks everywhere. I've tried everything - sock baskets, sock drawers, even burning them. He just buys more socks. What should I do?

- Sockless in Seattle

Gabby Says:

Dear Sockless, Have you considered that this isn't about socks? This is clearly a performance art piece about the ephemeral nature of human connection. Your boyfriend isn't messy; he's a postmodern philosopher using hosiery as metaphor for life's transient beauty. Either that or he's just lazy. Burn his shoes too and see what happens.

"Relationship advice is just guessing with more emotional baggage."
- Dr. Rebecca Martinez, Institute of Obvious Relationship Studies
